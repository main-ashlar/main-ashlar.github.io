How to Play

Objective

The objective of the game is to navigate through rooms until reaching a boss stage, where defeating the boss takes you to a new level. The rooms are alligned in a maze-like pattern, so that the final boss room is at the end of the "maze".

How to Defeat Enemies

Enemies are defeated or killed after being clicked on a certain amount of times.

How to Advance to the Next Room

After defeating a certain amount of monsters in any normal room, gateways will appear, and clicking on any gateway will teleport you to the next room. However, going the same way in all rooms will not actually lead you in the same direction. Some rooms may be rotated in a different direction without you knowing, and therefore the "forward" door may actually be a "side" or "back" door.
